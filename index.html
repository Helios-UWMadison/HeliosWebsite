We are Helios

A renewable energy student org committed towards impactful and meaningful projects

  • Solar Panels On Gordons
  • Solar Bus Stop
  • Keeping Track of Solar Panel's Data
  • Creating Cleaner energy for Madison

Helios, a student led organization at UW-Madison, is focused on working on various projects to a) help create an impact in the city in the field of renewable energy and b) get more and more students involved in the process.

We work with students and University faculty of all fields and majors, from STEM to the Humanities, to accomplish these ambitious goals. If you enjoy working on various projects, want to create an impact on our University, and enjoy pizza, this might be the right club for you!
